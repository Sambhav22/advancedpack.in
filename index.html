About Us

"Advanced Print-o-Pack is a venture of UGS Group. We being in the business of printing and publishing for over one and a half decades, the longevity and level of expertise of the group is an asset in providing our customers quality packaging solution. We aim to deliver high quality folding cartons across different industry segments."
At APP, we work on building true customer partnership by delivering quality in our products and services, being innovative in our approach and processes, and always following fair business practices with honesty and integrity. APP combines its technical expertise with market insights to deliver customised solutions.

Our Vision

"To be a globally recognised brand with a distinct leadreship position in the packaging industry,as being the largest supplier of the high quality folding cartons."

Our Mission

"to generate sustainable stakeholder value by: becoming the preferred packaging solutions provider, working hard every day and striving for excellence in our endeavours. ,delivering superior customer service and quality. "
